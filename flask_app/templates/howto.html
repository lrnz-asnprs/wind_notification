Wind Notification for Sydvestpynten 🤘

Currently, we only support wind notifications for the spot Sydvestpynten. If the wind speed at this spot will be on average above 8 m/s for a 3 hour window, you will receive an email. The wind forecast data is coming from the Danish Marine Forecast (fcoo.dk).
